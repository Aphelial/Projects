Amenities


Luxurious Accommodations

Ocean-view suites: Spacious, elegantly designed with floor-to-ceiling windows offering breathtaking views of the ocean and sunset.

Private villas: Secluded villas with plunge pools, sun decks, and direct beach access for an intimate and serene experience.

Modern comforts: Each room is equipped with premium bedding, smart TVs, high-speed Wi-Fi, climate control, and spa-like bathrooms with rain showers.


Infinity Pool

A stunning infinity pool that merges with the horizon, providing panoramic views of the coastline and sunsets. Poolside cabanas and a swim-up bar serve refreshing cocktails and snacks throughout the day.


Spa and Wellness Center

A full-service spa offering a variety of rejuvenating treatments including massages, facials, body scrubs, and aromatherapy sessions.

Wellness programs including yoga, meditation, and holistic therapies aimed at promoting relaxation and mindfulness.

Indoor and outdoor spaces designed for tranquility, with natural elements to enhance the experience.


Dining

Sunset Grill & Lounge: The resort’s signature restaurant offers gourmet cuisine with a focus on fresh, locally sourced ingredients. Specializes in seafood, seasonal dishes, and international flavors.

Alfresco Beach Café: A casual beachside café serving light bites, tropical cocktails, and fresh juices with a view of the ocean.

In-room dining: 24-hour room service offering a wide selection of meals, drinks, and snacks for guests to enjoy in the comfort of their rooms.


Beachfront Access

Direct access to a private, white-sand beach with lounge chairs, umbrellas, and towel service

Water sports equipment available for guest use, including kayaks, paddleboards, and snorkeling gear.

Private beachside cabanas with personalized butler service for ultimate relaxation.


Adventure & Recreation

Guided nature hikes: Explore nearby trails, mountains, and forests with guided tours that showcase the local flora, fauna, and hidden gems.

Water sports center: Offering scuba diving, snorkeling, windsurfing, and jet ski rentals for the adventurous traveler.

Fitness center: A state-of-the-art gym with cardio machines, free weights, and group fitness classes available daily.

Lilac Sunset Escape Resort blends luxurious comfort with immersive experiences, providing every guest with a tailor-made escape in an idyllic setting.
